Info

Hi I'm NRKey and I operate a YouTube channel that mainly makes english covers of anime songs using vocal synths. I started gaining an interest in Vocaloid around 2019 and since then I moved on to the vocal synthesis software SynthesizerV for most of my productions. I cover a variety of genres on this channel and I hope to graduaully improve it as time goes on.

Join me as I combine AI and programming to create music!




What is SynthV?

SynthesizerV is a vocal synthesis software developed by the company Dreamtonics. It contains a variety of vocals to choose from and it currently supports the languages English, Japanese, Chinese/Cantonese.

One of the popular features of SynthV is it's AI engine making the vocals capable of singing in a life-like manner which I use in my music.

The program contains both male and female vocals with a variety of ranges and singing styles.
